Our Journey

Finding Each Other (2022)

Being single felt like freedom for us, but fate had other plans. In mid-2022 ✨a magical moment✨ happened. At that time, our mutual friend shared photos when He spent time with each of us, and boom—unexpectedly, we were both interested in getting to know each other.

Living the Relationship (2022)

After officially getting to know each other and becoming familiar with one another, exactly one month later, we finally met in Bandung City. Feeling that we were a good match, at the end of that meeting, we decided to start a relationship on September 2, 2024, in a long-distance relationship (LDR)

Journey to the Wedding

Getting Engaged & Married (2024)

We have always committed to making time to meet each other every month, eventually, we're doing great with the LDR condition for 2 years, and finally we held our engagement ceremony on September 15, 2024, which concluded with setting the wedding date on November 24, 2024💍
